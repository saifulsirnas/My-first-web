Your donations can help us to support children affected by undernutrition,making sure that therapeutic milk is available at the nutrition facility and ready for distribution

Shelter for a family

Your donations can protect children and families against waterborne diseases through the immediate supply of hygiene kits jerrycans and water purification tablets that provide immediate relief on the ground

Your donations also help us support continued education for children during and after floods and cyclones providing learning support via makeshift school and distributing emergency learning kits.
